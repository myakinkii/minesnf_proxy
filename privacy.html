This privacy policy governs your use of the minesnf.com website ("Website") as well as android and ios versions of minesnf mobile application ("Application") that was created by Alex Myakinkii as an Open Source app.

What information does the Website/Application obtain and use?

Neither the Websitte nor the Application do collect or transmit any personally identifiable information about you, such as your name, address, phone number or email address.

The Website/Application does not use or collect any data related to your geographic location.

Do advertising companies, analytics providers, or any other third parties collect and use my data?

The Website/Application has no facility for collecting, transmitting, or maintaining user data, so no data is shared with any third parties.

How is user identified by backend when playing multiplayer modes?

Anonymous temporary session is created on the backend every time you connect to the backend. Only this session identificator is used as a cookie by the Website/Application.

Is some data stored on your device?

Yes, browser local storage is used to persist some player profile data

Why does the Application need camera access?

The Application needs camera access to provide you with a profile import feature via qr scanner
